Smart Contracts
In the current healthcare environment, most do not know the terms of the deal and more often than not one centralzied party can dictate those terms and change them at will. By utilzing smart contracts, there is a mathematical proof that the money for a healthcare transaction stays where it's suppossed to, and the terms are hard coded so no one party can decide to change them at will. This is a revolutionairy step so that patients know they are getting the care they pay for, and providers know when they are getting paid and for how much.
